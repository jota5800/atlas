About Me

I started climbing a few years ago and it quickly became one of my favorite ways to stay active. I mainly boulder but sometimes go outdoors when the weather is good. I like the mix of problem-solving and physical challenge—it keeps every session different.

Recent Routes

  • Sidepull Alley (V3): technical slab that helped me trust smaller holds.
  • Pocket Math (V8): overhang route that taught me better body positioning.
  • Topout Nerves (V9): simple but great for working on balance and control.

Gear I Use

  • Climbing shoes with a strong edge for precision
  • Chalk bag and loose chalk
  • Finger tape and hand balm
  • Small brush for cleaning holds
